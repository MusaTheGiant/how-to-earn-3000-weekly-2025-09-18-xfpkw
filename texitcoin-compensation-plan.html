How the Rapid Rewards Compensation Plan works

Binary Matching System

Each affiliate tracking center contains two legs: left and right. Every new package sold in your organization earns one point in the leg where it is placed. Points accumulate unlimited depth. When points match between left and right, commissions are paid on matched sets.

6 / 6$2,000
9 / 9$3,000 (max per center)

Points, Spillover, and Rollover

You earn points for direct referrals and for team placements from spillover. Unmatched points rollover to the next commission period. The system encourages consistent weekly growth so you can scale over time.
