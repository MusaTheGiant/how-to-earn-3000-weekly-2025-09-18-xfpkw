At TexitCoin, we believe in one simple principle: there are no free rides. Rewards must be earned through real personal effort and contribution.

Commission Potential is the total amount of commission you can withdraw, based on your personal sponsorships.

How It Works:

  • Each purchased seat you personally sponsor = 1 point
  • Every point = $1,000 in Commission Potential
  • Bonus seats (like 1-2-Free rewards) do NOT generate points
  • Each commission payout deducts from your total Commission Potential

🌟 The Good News: Once you're recognized as a TEXIT Ranger, this cap is LIFTED completely - meaning unlimited earning potential!

You can never take more commission than you've personally brought into our community. This structure ensures that personal effort sets the ceiling on your commission rewards, and team growth accelerates you toward reaching that ceiling. The more you sponsor and build personally, the higher your Commission Potential grows.
