⛏️WHAT IS TXC

What is TexitCoin (TXC)?

TexitCoin (TXC) is a Layer 1, proof-of-work cryptocurrency built on the proven Scrypt algorithm, designed for fast, low-cost transactions and completely fair distribution. Created by cryptocurrency pioneer Bobby Gray in 2024, TXC represents more than just digital currency. It's a movement toward financial sovereignty and decentralized economic empowerment.

Unlike many cryptocurrencies with questionable origins, TexitCoin started with zero pre-mine, ensuring every participant begins on equal footing. With new blocks created every 3 minutes and a mining lifespan designed to last over 138 years, TXC combines the security of Bitcoin with the efficiency of modern blockchain technology.
