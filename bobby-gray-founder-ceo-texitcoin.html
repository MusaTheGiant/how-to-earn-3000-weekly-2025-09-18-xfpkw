⚡ABOUT BOBBY

Visionary Leader in Cryptocurrency Innovation

Bobby Gray stands as one of the most experienced and respected figures in the cryptocurrency and alternative currency space. With over 16 years of expertise in precious metals and a pioneering role in early Bitcoin development, Bobby brings unmatched credibility and vision to the digital currency revolution.

As the Founder and CEO of TexitCoin (TXC), Bobby is committed to creating a fast, cheap, and superior alternative to Bitcoin that serves Texas by promoting economic sovereignty and local control through decentralized digital currency.

⚡ACHIEVEMENTS

Groundbreaking Achievements

Early Bitcoin Pioneer

In 2011, Bobby created the original physical Bitcoin coin, becoming one of the first innovators to bridge the gap between traditional physical currency and digital assets. This groundbreaking work established him as a true pioneer in cryptocurrency adoption.

Original Physical Bitcoin Creator (2011) Bitcoin Cold Storage Innovator (2017) Congressional Expert Witness (2012)

Monetary Policy Expertise

Bobby founded the American Open Currency Standard in 2008, demonstrating his early recognition of the need for alternative monetary systems. His expertise was formally recognized when he testified as an expert witness before the U.S. Congressional Domestic Monetary Services Subcommittee in 2012.

This rare honor reflects his deep understanding of monetary policy and alternative currencies, positioning him as a trusted authority in the field long before cryptocurrency gained mainstream recognition.

⚡GLOBAL EXPERIENCE

Global Cryptocurrency Experience

International Market Insights

Bobby's global perspective was shaped by his time in South Korea during the 2017 cryptocurrency mania, where he witnessed firsthand the explosive growth and adoption of digital currencies in international markets.

His years living in Singapore and Thailand provided valuable insights into Asian cryptocurrency markets and regulatory environments, experiences that now inform TexitCoin's strategic development and global positioning.

TexitCoin represents the culmination of decades of experience in monetary policy, precious metals, and cryptocurrency innovation - designed specifically to serve Texas and promote true financial independence.
⚡TXC VISION

The TexitCoin Revolution

Building Financial Sovereignty

After returning to Texas, Bobby launched TexitCoin with a clear mission: to serve Texas by promoting economic sovereignty and local control through a decentralized digital currency that's faster, cheaper, and more practical than Bitcoin.

TexitCoin features no pre-mining, transparent mining operations exclusively in Texas, and focuses on being a usable currency rather than just a speculative asset. This approach reflects Bobby's commitment to creating real value for the Texas community.

Commitment to Transparency

Bobby emphasizes complete transparency in TexitCoin's mining operations, with all mining infrastructure located exclusively in Texas. This commitment to regional pride and financial independence sets TexitCoin apart from other cryptocurrency projects.

Under Bobby's leadership, TexitCoin is actively expanding its mining infrastructure and merchant adoption throughout Texas, creating a robust ecosystem for practical cryptocurrency use.
